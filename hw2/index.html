Musical Flight

Homework 2 Visualizer

 

Description

Musical Flight is an audio visualizer where birds follow the sound of music.

Watch on YouTube

Instructions

How to run:

```


        $ chuck hw2_main.ck

```

Built on MacOS M1

 

Reflection

The goal of this visualizer was to have each bird that landed on the wire represent a musical voice. Given more time, I would have liked there to be more birds landing and taking off from the wire at various intervals, with "voices" coming and going more frequently. Additionally, I hardcoded the music and I wish that there were more random elements to it, such as birds flying by adding in little melodic lines that are generated. 

This project took so much longer than I anticipated... As I stated in the previous week's reading response, I spent too much time trying to add little flourishes in the beginning when I needed to just get the proof-of-concept down for the full narrative.

Regarding difficulties... there were a TON. My envelope follower worked up until yesterday, no idea why it stopped working. It's pretty much just the code Kunwoo provided us; I tried debugging it for a solid 30 minutes trying all sorts of fixes (even setting it back to its initial state from the milestone, where it worked) and it just wouldn't give me a non-null result. A weird issue I just encountered hours before the due date is that the program only runs smoothly when it is played from my second external monitor: if it's played from my Mac laptop display or my primary external monitor display I experience terrible performance issues. No idea why that is, and I'm not going to question it as long as some form of it works. 

Overall I am happy with the results even if I only implemented about 40% of what I wanted to do.
